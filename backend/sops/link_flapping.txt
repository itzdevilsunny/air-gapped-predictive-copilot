Troubleshooting link flapping and routing flaps in SD-WAN tunnels over MPLS.
Link status toggling (flapping) is often caused by OSPF Hello interval mismatch or MTU fragmentation.
To resolve routing instability, verify MTU size is 1500 (or 1400 on tunnels) and shut down the unstable primary interface to reroute traffic to the secondary backup path: 'interface GigabitEthernet0/1; shutdown' and 'interface GigabitEthernet0/2; no shutdown'.
